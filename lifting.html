Custom Lifting Devices and DNV-Certified Portable Offshore Units

Well Engineered specializes in custom lifting devices for offshore applications in Nova Scotia, Canada. When standard solutions fall short, we design and supply custom lifting devices in aluminum and steel, meeting ASME BTH-1 and ASME B30 standards.

Our team also assesses and evaluates existing lifting devices, ensuring compliance and safety for each unique application. Contact us for DNV-certified Portable Offshore Units (POUs) built to withstand harsh marine environments and meet DNV Standard for Certification 2.7-3. We manage every detail, from design to certification, documentation, and testing, to ensure your POU is ready for shipping quickly and efficiently.

DNV Portable Offshore Units

Contact Well Engineered for your DNV certified Portable Offshore Units (POUs). We will work with you to assess your requirements and design a POU to withstand the harshest marine conditions. We will ensure the design meets DNV Standard for Certification 2.7-3 and will engage a certifying authority to get your POU approved. Let us take care of arranging all necessary documentation, drawings, and testing to ensure your POU is ready for shipping as soon as possible.
