Why Post Frame?

Well Engineered is offering design/build services for post-framed wooden buildings with spans up to 80 feet, ceiling heights up to 30 feet, and located near to the HRM. This is a versatile and cost-effective building method that, unlike traditional methods, uses fewer materials resulting in lower construction time and lower overall cost.

  • Cost-Effective and Efficient Construction:
    • Post-frame buildings use fewer materials, reducing construction time and overall costs.
  • Lower Carbon Footprint:
    • Compared to steel or concrete buildings, post-frame construction is more environmentally friendly.
  • Structural Flexibility and Clear Spans:
    • Clear spans up to 80 feet and ceiling heights up to 30 feet allow for open, versatile interiors without interior support walls.
  • Thermal Performance and Energy Efficiency:
    • With minimal thermal bridging and insulated sheathing systems, post-frame buildings have excellent thermal performance and low energy consumption.
  • Ease of Modification and Maintenance:
    • The design allows for future modifications, including enclosing overhangs for extra interior space and easy roof maintenance.
  • Fire Resistance and Aesthetic Appeal:
    • Heavy wood posts can be partially exposed for a rustic look while maintaining fire resistance, and steel cladding creates a durable, non-combustible building envelope.

Beam Design and Installation

If you’re looking to make your living space open-concept, we are experienced at installing beams to replace load-bearing walls. If a seamless look is more your style, ask about our hidden beam design, we can cut your joists and sink a beam into your ceiling for that open feel. If you’re looking for a focal point consider our flitch beam design. The wood and steel sandwich structure allows for a lower profile structure than wood alone, without having to cut any ceiling joists.
