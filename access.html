SkyWalk Floating Ramp System

The Skywalk system is a versatile commercial access structure designed for durability and adaptability. It can include heated stairs for enhanced safety and convenience, and it offers configurations such as ramps, stairs, or a combination of both. Built to last, the system is fully salt-resistant and can be tailored to specific needs, with options for fully non-combustible materials or a hybrid steel, wood, and concrete design like the one used at the daycare. For improved accessibility, stairs can be configured with a low rise and long run, creating a safer climbing experience. The primary structure features galvanized steel with concrete walking surfaces, while optional covered pressure-treated columns add durability. The secondary structure, including guards and handrails, can be customized in wood or aluminum for a functional yet aesthetic finish.

Enhanced Safety in All Weather Conditions

Optional heated surfaces on ramps and stairs prevent ice and snow buildup, significantly reducing slip hazards. This makes the Skywalk system ideal for use in regions with harsh winters, ensuring year-round safety and usability.

Energy-Efficient Heating Solutions

The integrated heating system is designed for efficient energy usage, keeping operational costs manageable while maintaining optimal safety. This feature ensures the Skywalk is both practical and sustainable for long-term use.

Adaptable Safety Features

Custom configurations, such as low-rise, long-run stairs for easier climbing and optional heated surfaces, ensure the Skywalk system not only meets but exceeds safety and accessibility standards. This makes it suitable for diverse applications, from commercial to industrial spaces.
