About Well Engineered

Well Engineered is a Halifax-based multi-disciplinary engineering, manufacturing, and construction company. Our professional employees are passionate about their work, and have experience spanning many fields and projects. We excel in areas where there isn't an established presence in the marketplace - we do well at things that others don't do, and also where there is a benefit to integrating most or all aspects of a job.

Our staff are designers at heart - innovation is woven into our business processes. We may not be competitive for a 12-foot span beam design and install, but we have the expertise to replace 30 feet of load-bearing wall to open up the main floor of a house. We don't build standard buildings like tilt-up or pre-engineered steel - but we will design and build a modern post-framed building with better energy performance and a much lower carbon footprint.

We've designed products for third parties from scratch that our customers have built multi-million dollar businesses from. We are innovators with diverse, direct experience in most engineering disciplines.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to create a workplace for employees that is rewarding, fun, and respectful to all. We will enthusiastically pursue work that our staff enjoy, which leads to our people doing their best work for your project.

Innovation

The foundation of the majority of our employees is engineering design. We will not hesitate to innovate to join ideas or functions, often this happens organically on the fly during a project. The result is a nimble development and execution of a project, and a surprisingly swift resolution of problems.

Quality

Plan - Do - Check - Act is the core of our ISO9001:2015 certified quality system, which has framework for all processes at Well Engineered. We are keen to evolve it as well, with a large focus on continuous improvement using out home-grown non-punitive reporting and implementation tool.

Customer Focus

We strive to meet your expectations as a customer, our aim is to develop a relationship that will result in ongoing work for years to come. We will make our best effort to outline expectations at the outset, and hold ourselves to them during the project. While the goal is to work without errors, we do recognize that errors happen in all things - what matters is how one reacts afterwards!
