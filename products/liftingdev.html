Below The Hook Lifting Devices

Well Engineered has designed and manufactured many ASME B30 compliant lifting devices to handle various materials for a wide range of equipment and materials. We have a lifter for pipes, a lifter for paper rolls, bags of sand, railcar axles, all the way to full sized aircraft. We can design lightweight lifters from aluminum, corrosion-resistant stainless steel, or more conventional steel.
