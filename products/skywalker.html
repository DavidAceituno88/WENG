Floating Ramp System

SkyWalk

The Skywalk system is built to last. Fully salt-resistant, and optional heated surfaces this access system can be configured with a ramp alone, stairs alone, or both. For ease of climbing we can configure stairs with a low rise and long run, resulting in a safer and more accessible staircase. All walking surfaces are concrete, primary structure is galvanized steel with optional covered pressure treated columns. Secondary structure (guard and handrail) can be wood or aluminum.
