Commercial Access System

SkyWalk

The Skywalk system is built to last. Fully salt-resistant, and optional heated surfaces this access system can be configured with a ramp alone, stairs alone, or both. For ease of climbing we can configure stairs with a low rise and long run, resulting in a safer and more accessible staircase. All walking surfaces are concrete, primary structure is galvanized steel with optional covered pressure treated columns. Secondary structure (guard and handrail) can be wood or aluminum.

Key Features

  • Easily configured for any elevation, ramp sections and staircases are made-to-order to fit your site.
  • All walking and rolling surfaces are concrete, optionally heated, optionally tinted for colour.
  • Design life is 40 years. Warranty is 10 years in commercial applications.
  • Permanent installation on frost-resistant footings.
  • Optional long run and low rise for easy climbing for those with mobility restrictions.
  • Fully ADA and NBCC 2020 compliant.
  • Resistant to salt and freeze/thaw cycles.
  • Configurable stair widths as required for your application, including centre rail when required by code.
  • Maintenance-free.
  • Fully non-combustible construction is optional.
  • Rails and guards can be all wood, all aluminium, or both depending on desired aesthetics.
